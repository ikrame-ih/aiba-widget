## Extracted design system

- **Type:** Outfit, generous clock size, compact labels in uppercase tracking
- **Shape language:** 24px outer radius, restrained controls, cool paper surfaces
- **Color:** Tricorn Black and cool ivory, functional red, Aiba blue, minimal gold
- **Mascot stage:** large reference-faithful silhouette, dedicated corridor in expanded mode
- **Workspace:** one active surface, no repeated day panels or nested card stacks
- **Motion:** short panel fade, companion breathing, reduced-motion fallback

The native windows use **400×480** (compact) and **900×720** (expanded) content bounds.
